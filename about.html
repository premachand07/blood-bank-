About Us

DONATION DESK is a website with the aim of providing a safe platform for blood donation management system.

This initiative is taken by a team of 4 second year computer science engineering students.

This website is still under development.



Our Mission



  1. To develop a safe platform for blood donation management
  2. To ensure a reliable communication between donors and patients keeping privacy.
  3. To make it easier to communicate with specialized doctors.
  4. To allow users contacting admins directly in emergency.
  5. To provide a dynamic database that is storing donors' information and communicate with them easily
